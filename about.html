About Us

Who Are We?

The founders of of Forest Unite is just a group of young students who lives somewhere in Marinduque. Back then before, they already engages in school extra curricular activities regarding on environment conservation.

They noticed that people nowadays especially the young ones, doesn't really care much anymore in terms of taking care of the environment. So they decided to start a small non-profit organization. They only started with just only 10 members, but because of full dedication and passion to the environment, the organization gradually gathered their own success, experiences and volunteers.

Today, they already have about 350 members across Marinduque, with over hundred successful projects.

Our History

With an ongoing news regarding to the destruction of nature, and its effects to the environment. It opened the eyes of a young students to build their own small organization, with an aim of making changes of restoring the forests as well as educating other people about it.

These young students decided to name their organization into Forest Unite that was established in the early 2020. Later on, this organization became well known as Forest Unite, who already successfully accomplished a lot of projects, and able to encourage other volunteers.

As of today, Forest Unite still continuing their activities like how they started before, and they keep pushing themselves for the sake of the environment. And will continue to pass their aim to the future generations.
